About us

Who Are We?

We are a dynamic for impact organization committed to fostering holistic community-impact in Zimbabwe.

We strive to uplift the lives of vulnerable and underprivilege individuals, catalyzing positve change, not only for them but for their entire communities.

Through innovative and sustainable approaches, we address pressing social issues such as poverty, health disparities, and educational inequities, working tirelessly to create a future where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

Welcome to Swizimtrust

We are a dynamic organization dedicated to creating holistic community impact in Zimbabwe.

Smart Agriculture & Sustainability

We promote sustainable, climate-smart agricultural practices to improve food security and income, conserve natural resources, and empower farmers with the knowledge and tools needed for long-term productivity and resilience.

Psycho-social support

We prioritize both material and psychosocial needs, creating spaces where individuals receive essential resources and emotional support to reach their full potential and lead fulfilling lives.

Community Health & Safety

We prioritize community well-being through health and safety programs, including menstrual health education, support for single mothers and pregnant teens, and assistance for child-headed homes to ensure children's access to education.

Entrepreneurship & Social

We empower individuals through entrepreneurship training and education at our Nooks, providing skills, mentorship, and practical knowledge to help them succeed and create sustainable livelihoods.
